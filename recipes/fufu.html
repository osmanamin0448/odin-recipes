Fufu is a staple, starchy dough from West Africa, particularly iconic in Ghana, made by pounding boiled cassava and unripe plantains (or cocoyam) into a smooth, elastic paste.

Ingredients

  • Cassava
  • Green plaintain
  • Water

Steps

  1. Peel and wash cassava and plantain
  2. Remove fiber woody from middle of cassava chunks
  3. Place the cassava and plantain in a pot and conver with water then boil for sometime
  4. Drain the water
